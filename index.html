About me

I'm a Cybersecurity undergraduate at Multimedia University, passionate about building secure, real-world systems — from full-stack web applications to intelligent, data-driven platforms. My work sits at the intersection of software development and security.

My projects reflect that range. I built GovCare+, a multilingual government complaint routing system powered by NLP (BERT). I work with tools like Kali Linux, Wireshark, and OWASP ZAP — driven by the mindset that good software isn't just built, it's built to withstand the unexpected.
